We're part of the community and we want our taproom to feel like your second living room. When we're not in the brewhouse, you might find us hiking in the State Park or fishing out on the lake.

Hospitable

Whether its in the taproom or the biergarten, you're sure to find a nice spot to sip. Looking for a venue for your next event? Ask us about renting the farmhouse or the taproom. We can host up to 100 guests.

We're proud to be the first craft brewery in Columbus county.

Our Beer

From Double IPA's to easy-drinking Lagers, we offer a variety of brews to suit your fancy. Our permanent drafts are the Lakeshore Lager, Alligator Ale, Crusoe IPA, and Wananish Witbier. An addition to the standards, you can find seasonal offerings like our ever-popular Oktoberfest, limited-edition Chocolate Stout, or the Summertime Session. Our taps rotate weekly so stop by and see what's new!

Blending southern tradition with a modern aesthetic, everything we do is inspired by the land.
